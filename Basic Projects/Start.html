ABOUT BWASI

The Bay Area Women's Sport Initiative (BAWSI) is a public benefit, non-profit corparation with a mission to create programs through which women athletes bring health, hope and wholeness to our community.Founded in 2005 by Olympic and World Cup Soccer stars Brandi Chastain and Julie Foudy and MArlene Bjornsrud, former general manager of the San Jose CyberRays Women's profesional soccer team, BAWSI provides a meaningful path for women athelete to become amore vissible and valued part of the Bay Area Sport Culture.
